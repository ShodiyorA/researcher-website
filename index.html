  • The Impact of the TRIPS Agreement on copyright law harmonization: A Comparative study of Nordic States

    It is my PG Thesis focused on the theme: The Impact of the TRIPS Agreement on copyright law harmonization: A Comparative study of Nordic States.

    Read More This thesis aims to study how the TRIPS Agreement influences copyright law harmonization of Nordic Countries (Denmark, Finland, Iceland, Norway and Sweden). The main core of the thesis focus is to understand whether the TRIPS agreement leads to divergence or convergence within copyright regulations of these countries whereas it assesses wider impact on economic development, international trade as well as local creative fields. The thesis also includes a comprehensive and comparative analysis of Nordic Countries’ copyright law before and after the implementation of the TRIPS Agreement and challenges and benefits of the TRIPS Agreement to national copyright laws in harmonization. Moreover, the role of the jurisdiction of European Union is not deniable and it will be analyzed in terms of Nordic EU member countries (Sweden, Finland and Denmark) and how digital advancement has impacts on copyright protection.
